About Me

My name is Brian, I'm a Full(er)-Stack Engineer. Why? Because I spent almost 10 years as a graphic designer in the world of concerts and entertainment and now I'm ready to solve some different kinds of problems! After dabbling in Python and basic web developement, I finally decided to switch careers in 2020 to combine problem solving, technology and creativity and joined General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Program, a 12-week bootcamp to begin my career as a Software Developer. Now, I can code your back-end, code your front-end, AND design it all too! I love challenges and rarely back down from one (that's how I ended up running my first marathon) so let's solve some problems together!

You can also find me making music, climbing, traveling, and eating. Mostly eating...
